Company Overview

What is ReadyPi?

Ready + API + π — infinite access, ready now

ReadyPi is Bangladesh's first AI API aggregation platform — the single key that unlocks 50+ AI models for every developer, freelancer, and builder in South Asia, payable entirely in BDT without an international card.

ReadyPi is built and operated by Rareware Studio, a technology studio based in Sylhet, Bangladesh. The platform was conceived in April 2026 to solve the most persistent infrastructure gap in Bangladesh's booming developer economy: the payment wall that separates 600,000+ BD developers from the AI APIs they need to build world-class products.

The name carries three layers of meaning. Ready — because the API is always live, always accessible, requiring no workaround. PI — a nod to the infinite nature of π, representing the infinite scale of AI models and the mathematical precision of our pricing engine. And the hidden API embedded in the name itself — because ReadyPi is, at its core, an API-first platform built for builders.

Policy Framework

Rules We Live By

ReadyPi operates under a clear, transparent policy framework. Every rule exists to protect our users, our platform, and the broader BD developer community.

Acceptable Use Policy

1
No Harmful Content Generation

ReadyPi's API must not be used to generate content that incites violence, promotes hate speech, facilitates illegal activities, or harms individuals or groups. Accounts found in violation are suspended immediately without refund of remaining credits.

2
No API Reselling Without Authorization

Users may not resell ReadyPi API access to third parties without an explicit Reseller Agreement with Rareware Studio. Building SaaS products on top of ReadyPi is permitted and encouraged — reselling raw API keys is not.

3
One Account Per Legal Entity

Each individual or business entity is permitted one ReadyPi account. Creating multiple accounts to bypass free tier limits or abuse promotional credits is a violation. Detected duplicate accounts are merged or terminated.

4
No Automated Abuse or Scraping

Rate limits exist to protect platform stability for all users. Attempts to bypass rate limiting through automation, proxy rotation, or other technical means will result in immediate account suspension.

5
Credits Are Non-Refundable

Purchased credits and subscription fees are non-refundable as per standard SaaS practice. Credits never expire. In cases of platform error causing credit loss, ReadyPi will restore the affected amount upon verified report.

6
Privacy & Data Handling

ReadyPi logs API requests at the metadata level (model used, token count, timestamp) for billing purposes only. Prompt content and API responses are not stored beyond the request lifecycle. User data is never sold, shared with advertisers, or used for model training.

7
Uptime & SLA Commitment

ReadyPi targets 99.9% monthly uptime for all paid plans. Scheduled maintenance windows are announced 48 hours in advance via email and the status page. Pro and Team plan users receive compensation credits for unplanned downtime exceeding 1 hour.

8
Responsible AI Clause

ReadyPi routes requests to third-party AI providers (Anthropic, OpenAI, Google, etc.) whose own usage policies govern model behaviour. Users are jointly responsible for ensuring their use of ReadyPi-routed AI complies with both ReadyPi's policies and the underlying provider's terms of service.

Security Policy

All ReadyPi API keys are generated with cryptographic randomness and stored as bcrypt hashes — we cannot recover your key if lost, only regenerate it. Keys begin with the prefix rpi_ for identification. All platform traffic is encrypted via TLS 1.3. Payment data is never stored on ReadyPi servers — all transactions are processed by SSLCommerz, NOWPayments, and their respective PCI-DSS certified infrastructure.
